Frequently Asked Questions

What is SelfHelp AI?

SelfHelp AI is your personal AI life coach, available 24/7 to help you achieve your personal growth goals through personalized conversations and actionable guidance.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your personal information and chat history is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any central database of user information.

How do I start using the app?

Simply complete the initial survey to personalize your coaching experience, and you can start chatting with your AI coach immediately.

Can I change my preferences?

Yes, you can modify your coaching style, focus areas, and other preferences through the settings menu at any time.
